Instructions

  • Pan: Click and drag the canvas background.
  • Deselect: Click the canvas background.
  • Multi-select: Hold Ctrl/Cmd or Shift while clicking nodes/edges.
  • Copy: Select items and press Ctrl/Cmd+C.
  • Cut: Select items and press Ctrl/Cmd+X.
  • Paste: Press Ctrl/Cmd+V.
  • Undo: Press Ctrl/Cmd+Z.
  • Redo: Press Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+Z.
  • Context Menu: Right-click on a node or edge.
